Track Your Ones

TrackYourOnes is a minimalist style Android social app. The main purpose of this app is to track your dear ones and to be able to contact them. Similar to WhatsApp, you can track your dear ones' locations, see their statuses and even contact them by sending messages. This app has not yet been released since there is a couple of minor changes to be made. I will be releasing this very soon.
